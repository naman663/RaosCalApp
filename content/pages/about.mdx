## Our Mission

The Calorie Tracking App is designed to make nutrition tracking simple, intuitive, and effective. We believe that understanding what you eat is the first step toward a healthier lifestyle, and our goal is to empower users with the tools they need to make informed dietary decisions.

## What We Do

Our platform provides a comprehensive suite of tools for tracking your daily calorie intake and nutritional habits:

- **Food Logging** — Quickly log meals and snacks with our extensive food database.
- **Nutritional Insights** — Get detailed breakdowns of macronutrients, vitamins, and minerals.
- **Goal Setting** — Set personalized calorie and nutrition goals based on your health objectives.
- **Progress Tracking** — Visualize your progress over time with intuitive charts and reports.
- **Meal Planning** — Plan your meals ahead of time to stay on track with your goals.

## Why Calorie Tracking Matters

Research consistently shows that people who track their food intake are more successful at achieving and maintaining a healthy weight. By bringing awareness to your eating habits, you can:

1. Identify patterns and areas for improvement in your diet.
2. Make more mindful choices about what and how much you eat.
3. Ensure you are meeting your nutritional needs.
4. Stay accountable to your health and fitness goals.

## Our Approach

We built this project on top of modern web technologies to deliver a fast, reliable, and enjoyable user experience:

- **Next.js 13** — Leveraging the latest in server components and the app router for optimal performance.
- **Prisma ORM** — Providing a robust and type-safe data layer.
- **Tailwind CSS** — Ensuring a clean and responsive design across all devices.
- **Radix UI** — Delivering accessible and composable UI components.
- **TypeScript** — Maintaining code quality and developer productivity with static typing.
